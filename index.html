My Favourite Footballer: Kylian Mbappé

About

Kylian Mbappé is a French professional footballer who plays as a forward for Paris Saint-Germain and the France national team. He is known for his incredible speed, dribbling, and finishing ability.

Quick Facts

Career Highlights

  • FIFA World Cup Winner (2018)
  • UEFA Champions League Finalist
  • Ligue 1 Top Scorer (multiple seasons)
  • Golden Boy Award (2017)
